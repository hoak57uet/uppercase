What is the Vai Syllabary?

The Vai syllabary is a unique writing system that represents the sounds and syllables of the Vai language. Some Vai characters resemble Latin letters, enhanced with artistic embellishments, offering a visually captivating way to express text.

The Significance of Vai Characters

Developed in the early 19th century by Momolu Duwalu Bukele, the Vai script serves as a crucial tool for preserving the Vai language. Added to the Unicode Standard in version 5.2 (October 2009), the Vai block allows for the digital representation of this important cultural heritage.
